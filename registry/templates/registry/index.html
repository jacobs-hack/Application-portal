Join the Jacobs Alumni Association e.V

In order to become a member of the Association, you need to provide some personal information about yourself, which will require about 10 minutes of your time.

If you are an existing member, please use this opportunity to update your data and enable the recurring payment of the membership fees. The fee for 2017 is due by the end of the year. The registration process will feel like a new application but rest assured that your Jacobs Alumni Google account remains intact.

Register Login

If you are registering with the Association for the first time, you will enjoy the following benefits:

  • Google account @jacobs-alumni.de e-mail and access to various apps
  • Unlimited Google Drive space
  • More easily connect with other alumni via our membership portal
  • Stay in touch with the University via regular newsletters
  • Career services in collaboration with the University
  • Invitations to University events and alumni events

You can choose one of the three membership tiers at the end of the registration process:

  • Contributor – Standard membership for 39€ p.a.
  • Patron – Premium membership (incl. additional benefits) for 249€ p.a.
  • Starter – Membership for fresh graduates (or those in financial need) for 0.01€ (1 symbolic cent) p.a.
